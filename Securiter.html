Security

Improving student life on campuses

Student life on campuses presents challenges impacting well-being and success:

  1. Inadequate security leading to theft, assault, or harassment.
  2. Isolation, loneliness, especially for distant or new students.
  3. Mental health issues from academic pressure and social expectations.
  4. Lack of affordable housing, healthy food, and transportation.
  5. Discrimination based on race, gender, or socioeconomic status.

To join the Security:

  1. Form a diverse team.
  2. Register on our website for access to resources.
  3. Attend the kickoff event for problem statement and brainstorming.
  4. Develop your solution with technology and design thinking.
  5. Present to judges and get feedback.
  6. Celebrate and network.
